About Us

TARUMT Internship Portal is dedicated to connecting talented students with valuable internship opportunities. Our mission is to empower the next generation of professionals by providing hands-on experience in various industries.

With a network of partner organizations, we offer a wide range of internship positions that cater to different interests and career goals. Whether you're a student seeking an internship or a company looking to hire interns, we're here to bridge the gap.
